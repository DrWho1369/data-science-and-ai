ğŸ“Œ Binomial Distribution

The binomial distribution models the number of successes in n independent Bernoulli trials.

1ï¸âƒ£ Characteristics of a Binomial Experiment

  • Fixed number of trials (n).
  • Each trial has only two possible outcomes (Success or Failure).
  • The probability of success (p) remains constant.
  • Trials are independent.

2ï¸âƒ£ Binomial Probability Formula

P(X = k) = (n choose k) p^k (1 - p)^(n - k)
  • n = number of trials
  • k = number of successes
  • p = probability of success
  • (n choose k) = "n choose k" (binomial coefficient)

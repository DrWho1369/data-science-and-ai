ğŸ“Œ Empirical Rule (For Normal Distributions)

For bell-shaped distributions, the Empirical Rule states:

  • 68% of data lies within 1 standard deviation from the mean.
  • 95% of data lies within 2 standard deviations.
  • 99.7% of data lies within 3 standard deviations.

ğŸ”¹ Application:

If a university entrance test has a mean score of 70 and SD of 10, then:

  • 68% of students score between 60 and 80.
  • 95% score between 50 and 90.
  • 99.7% score between 40 and 100.
